Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy crucial to our company. This Privacy Statement conveys our commitment to protecting your personal information and delivers transparency on how we collect, use, and secure your data.

Collecting Data

Personal Information:

When making use of our platform, we may assemble personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. This information is vital for processing bookings, facilitating transactions, and tailoring customer support to your needs.

Data Usage:

We collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website, including your IP address, browser type, and visited pages. This data helps us analyze user behavior, track trends, and enhance the functionality and performance of our platform.

Cookies:

Similar to other websites, we utilize cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and personalize your interactions with our site. Cookies enable us to remember your preferences, customize content, and deliver targeted advertising based on your interests.

Data Usage

Service Provision:

Your personal data is used to fulfil bookings, safely process payments, and offer seamless customer experiences. By gaining a deeper understanding of your preferences and past interactions, we can tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

Marketing:

With your consent, we may use your contact information to send promotional offers, newsletters, and updates about our latest deals and services. You have the option to opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

Analytics:

We analyze usage data and cookies to gain insights into user behavior, evaluate the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns, and enhance the overall user experience on our platform. This data enables us to identify areas for improvement and optimize our services accordingly.

Data Sharing

To effectively deliver our services, we may share your information with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment processors, booking platforms, and analytics partners. These partners are contractually obligated to maintain strict confidentiality and may only use your data for the purposes outlined in our agreements.

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to legal requests, court orders, or other legal processes. Additionally, we may share your data to protect our rights, property, or safety, as well as that of our users or others.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our security protocols include encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits to uphold the integrity and confidentiality of your data.

However, it’s important to note that while we take all reasonable precautions to safeguard your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security, and you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk when using our platform.

Your Rights

As a user of our platform, you have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, update, or delete your data. Additionally, you have the right to object to or restrict the processing of your information under specific circumstances.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal data or our Privacy Statement, please don’t hesitate to contact our dedicated support team. They are available to assist you and address any inquiries you may have.

Alterations to this Statement

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Statement at any time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any revisions will be posted on this page along with the effective date of the updated statement. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Statement to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.
